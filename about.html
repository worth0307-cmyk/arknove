Pulse

About

Pulse is a real-time salary calculator that turns your monthly paycheck into a live, second-by-second ticker. It exists for one simple reason: seeing your money tick up makes your work feel real.

The idea

Most of us get paid once a month and lose all visceral connection to how that number maps to our actual hours. Pulse closes that loop. Enter your salary, start the clock, and watch the abstract become concrete.

Built for focus

Pulse is intentionally minimal. One job, done well, with a tasteful design. No social features, no notifications, no manipulation. Just you and the ticker.

Pulse Pro

The core product is free. Pulse Pro is a $4.99 one-time upgrade for advanced features (themes, tax mode, daily goals, pause, custom milestones, no ads). One payment, lifetime access.
