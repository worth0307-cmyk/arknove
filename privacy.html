Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Pulse ("we", "us") handles information when you use our website.

What we collect

Pulse is a client-side calculator. The numbers you enter (salary, working hours, working days, tax rate, daily goal) are stored only in your own browser using localStorage. We do not transmit, collect, or have access to that information on any server.

We do not require account creation. We do not ask for your name, email, or personal contact information.

Cookies and similar technologies

We use a small number of cookies and localStorage entries for the following purposes:

  • Preferences: remembering your language, currency, theme, and recent inputs.
  • Consent: remembering your cookie choice so we do not ask again.
  • Pro status: remembering that you have purchased Pulse Pro.

Advertising

Free users see ads served by Google AdSense. Google and its third-party vendors use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google uses the DoubleClick DART cookie to serve ads based on a user's visit to our site and other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You may also opt out of third-party vendor cookies at aboutads.info.

If you decline non-essential cookies, ads may still appear but will not be personalized.

Children's privacy

Pulse is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will change accordingly.
