01 How it works

1
Enter your salary
Type your monthly take-home pay along with how many hours you work per day and how many days per month. Pulse uses these three numbers to calculate exactly how much you earn every second.
2
Clock in
When you start your work day, hit Clock In. A precise live ticker begins counting up — down to the millisecond — showing exactly how much money you've earned so far this session.
3
Celebrate milestones
Every few minutes you'll hit a milestone — a small celebration appears to mark the moment. After larger amounts, you get a full-screen money rain. Earning money should feel rewarding.
4
Clock out and review
When your day ends, clock out to see your total earnings, total time worked, milestones hit, and your effective hourly rate for this session.
